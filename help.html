How Me Content Works

A simple system for capturing, organizing, and executing your ideas.

Direction: Capture → Organize → Plan → Execute

1. Capture

Start here

Everything begins with a thought. Capture it immediately — video, text, or upload.

  • Record a video message to your future self.
  • Dump raw ideas without worrying about structure.
  • Attach screenshots, PDFs, or reference files.

2. Organize

Make it findable

Categories, tags, and types help your brain retrieve ideas instantly.

  • Use categories for platforms, projects, or themes.
  • Use tags for vibes, formats, or energy levels.
  • Use types to define the stage: idea, script, reminder, content.

3. Plan

Turn ideas into content

The pipeline and calendar help you move ideas toward execution.

  • Assign a status: Raw → Draft → Done.
  • Give serious ideas a publish date.
  • Use Today view to execute what matters now.

4. Reminders

Future you

Set a reminder date on any thought. It will appear in Today on that date.

Use reminders for ideas that feel important but not urgent.

Storage & Privacy

Local-first

Everything is stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your machine unless you export it.

  • LocalStorage keeps your data persistent.
  • You can export your entire vault in Settings.
  • Future versions may support cloud sync.
